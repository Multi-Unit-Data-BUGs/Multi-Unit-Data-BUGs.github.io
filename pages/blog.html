Our Urban Garden Experiment at the Loisaida Center

Posted on August 26, 2024 by Isaac Amar

We've been hard at work at Loisaida setting up our garden modules. During the setup, we identified a few areas for improvement: upgrading the mothership's power supply and enhancing LoRa connectivity, particularly given the challenges of an urban environment. We're also considering stronger antennas to boost signal strength and ensure stable data collection. Progress continues as we refine our setup for optimal performance.

Enclosure Advancements

Posted on August 31, 2024 by Spencer Kirsch

We're making steady progress on the enclosure front! We've created a frame to streamline soldering and compact our prototype. Additionally, we've developed a prebend fixture to efficiently connect wires between the Lora chip and ESP32 and refined the enclosure cap to fit our o-ring line perfectly. Next, we'll focus on finalizing the stake and enclosure design for the PCB, ensuring everything is robust and ready for deployment.
